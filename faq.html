Help

Q: How would I add another pet?
A: Just click on the plus sign at the top left of the navbar.

Q: Can I name my pet something ridiculous? (Yes, we’re looking at you, “The Chosen One”)
A: Of course you can! Label your pet whatever weird nickname your interesting heart desires.

Q: Will my pet remember me if I delete them or sign out?
A: You can sign back in anytime, and all your progress will be saved.

Q: What if I don’t want a starry pixelated background?
A: You can find the background changer in our settings.
